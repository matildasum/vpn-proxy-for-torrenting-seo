## FAQ

**Q: Is a vpn proxy for torrenting the same as a browser proxy?**  
A: No. Browser proxies often cover only one app. A VPN-style setup protects device traffic and is what you want for torrent clients.

**Q: Why is a Kill Switch non-negotiable for torrenting?**  
A: If the tunnel drops for even a second, your client can expose your real IP. Kill Switch cuts traffic instead.

**Q: Will this slow down my downloads?**  
A: Any VPN adds overhead, but with **High speed** routing and nearby **Global Servers**, the hit is usually small and the connection stays steadier.

**Q: What should I verify after connecting?**  
A: Confirm your public IP changed, then start torrents. If your client supports binding to the VPN adapter, use it.
